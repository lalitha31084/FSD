How to Make a Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread slices
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Take two slices of bread.
  2. Apply butter on one side of each slice.
  3. Prepare the vegetables:
    • Wash the lettuce
    • Slice the tomato
    • Grate the cheese
  4. Place lettuce, tomato, and cheese between the bread slices.
  5. Serve the sandwich.
